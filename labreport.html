Departments of BIM and BHM

The BIM (Bachelor in Information Management) and BHM (Bachelor in Hotel Management) departments at Sankalpa College provide quality edeucation in management, information systems, and hospitality studies.

Our departments focus on academic excellence and practical exposure. We aim to produce competent professionals with industry-ready skills.

Courses Offered

  1. Bachelor in Information Management (BIM)
    Integrates IT, management, and business studies.
  2. Bachelor in Hotel Management (BHM)
    Focuses on hospitality and tourism.
